About

National Level Technical Symposium

VARIT is a prestigious national level technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Applications, Jamal Mohamed College (Autonomous). This annual event brings together students, innovators, researchers, and industry experts from across the nation to showcase cutting-edge technology and foster innovation.

The symposium features a diverse range of activities including technical workshops, paper presentations, project exhibitions, coding competitions, and keynote speeches from renowned industry leaders. VARIT provides an excellent platform for students to demonstrate their technical prowess, exchange ideas, and network with peers and professionals.
